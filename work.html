  • Hong Kong Aging Project

  • Project brief

    The objective of this summer project was to reframe the understanding of the severe aging challenges that Hong Kong is facing, and to identify opportunity spaces associated with the future of aging on a system level.

  • What is the problem

    Hong Kong is aging fast. By 2050, The proportion of the population aged 65 and over is projected to become 1/3 of the entire population. However, Hong Kong is not prepared for the health, housing, and social welfare demands that come with such a growing population of senior adults.
